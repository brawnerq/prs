Play Rock Paper Scissors!

 

Rules

The game of P, R, S is based on trust, skill and determination.
  • In this version, you play against the bowser, who randomly selects his throw
  • The aren't any mental games to master here, it's purely luck
  • Here is the throw hierarchy:
  • Rock: wins against scissors, loses to paper and stalemates against itself
  • Paper wins against Rock, loses to scissors and stalemates against itself
  • Scissors wins against paper, loses to rock and stalemates against itself
